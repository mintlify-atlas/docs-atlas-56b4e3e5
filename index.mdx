This archive preserves all 300+ questions and detailed answers across frontend and backend engineering tracks — organized by topic so you can study exactly what you need.

## How questions are structured

Every question in this archive follows a consistent format designed to help you give strong interview answers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Question">
    The exact question as asked in real interviews at Korean tech companies.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Core answer">
    A clear, direct explanation of the concept — written to match the depth expected in a technical interview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Code examples">
    Where applicable, concrete code that illustrates the concept in action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow-up depth">
    Additional nuance, edge cases, and real-world considerations that distinguish strong candidates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This archive covers questions suitable for junior-to-senior frontend and backend engineers. Questions range from foundational concepts to advanced system design topics.
</Note>
